Privacy Policy

Wealth Tracker App

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wealth Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password.
  • Financial Data: Account names, balances, and portfolio information that you manually enter or sync from connected financial institutions.
  • Broker Credentials: If you choose to connect external accounts, we securely store encrypted credentials to access your financial data.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes, including device state, stack traces, and app version, collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us improve stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Wealth Tracker service
  • Display your financial portfolio and track your wealth over time
  • Sync data from connected financial institutions
  • Send you important notifications about your accounts
  • Improve and optimize our application
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Ensure the security of your account and our services

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data, including credentials and financial information, is encrypted both in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.
  • Secure Storage: Credentials are stored using industry-standard encryption (Fernet symmetric encryption).
  • Authentication: We use secure JWT-based authentication with regular token rotation.
  • Biometric Protection: The app supports biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint) for additional security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our app (e.g., cloud hosting, Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics, a service provided by Google, to collect crash reports and diagnostic information. This helps us identify and fix issues to improve app stability. Crashlytics collects:

  • Crash stack traces and exception information
  • Device model, OS version, and orientation
  • Available RAM and disk space at time of crash
  • A Crashlytics Installation UUID to identify unique app installations
  • App version and build number

Crashlytics does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or financial data. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

6.2 Financial Integrations

Our app integrates with third-party financial services to sync your account data:

  • Banking Integrations: We connect to banks via FinTS/HBCI protocols (for German banks) or direct API integrations.
  • Broker Integrations: We connect to investment platforms like Interactive Brokers, TrueWealth, VIAC, and Morgan Stanley.

When you connect an external account, you authorize us to access your financial data from that institution. We only retrieve the information necessary to display your balances and portfolio.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
